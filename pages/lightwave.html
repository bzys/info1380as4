Here are some 3D models made using NewTek LightWave.

NewTek LightWave is a 3D modelling program developed by NewTek, and has been used in film, television, motion graphics, digital matte painting, visual effects, video games development, product design, architectural visualizations, virtual production, music videos, pre-visualizations and advertising[Wikipedia].

I taught myself how to 3D model using LightWave in my senior year of highschool, and now uses it to make the models for use in the mods I make for some of the video games I play.
